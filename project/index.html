NewStore [link]

NewStore transforms brick and mortar shopping into app-powered, digitally connected retail experiences. Purpose built for the most demanding consumers, NewStore is the first to provide Omnichannel-as-a Service, integrating a cloud-based order management system and Mobile POS. With intuitive apps on the front-end for store employees and pre-built ecommerce connectors, retailers can easily offer Endless Aisle, Mobile Checkout, Omni Fulfillment, and Clienteling. NewStore was founded by Stephan Schambach, who revolutionized ecommerce by putting it in the cloud with Demandware (now Salesforce Commerce Cloud). The company is located in Boston, New York, and Berlin.

botlang [link]

A scripting language for conversational chat bots

The purpose of the botlang project is to design a simple, non-complex and user friendly comprehensive scripting language to enable non technical personnel in an organization to be able to implement and maintain chatbots, smart FAQ systems etc. Companies using the language benefit from rapid application time and therefore have the ability to reduce potential development costs for such system compared to a traditional approach.
